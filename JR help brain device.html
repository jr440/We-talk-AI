Our Mitigation:

Aggressive power management (keyword wake-up vs. always-on), realistic battery life claims (standby vs. continuous use), and shifting heavy AI tasks (like summarization) to an optional, premium cloud service.

Legal: The Law of Listening

Australian law requires all-party consent for recording private conversations. A passive recorder is legally untenable. Our solution is "Consent by Design."

Unambiguous Activation

Recording is initiated by a clear user action, accompanied by audible chimes and visible, non-disguisable lights to notify all parties.

Our Mitigation:

By embedding consent-facilitating features into the hardware and UI, we place the legal and ethical responsibility for lawful use with the user, mitigating company liability.
